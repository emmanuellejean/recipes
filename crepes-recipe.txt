CREPES recipe
ingredients
4 eggs
250g of flour
1/2 liter milk

pour the flour and form a volcano. 
add the eggs
and start to mix 
add little by little the milk

the mix should be liquid

preheat on high temperature a flat pan
grease your pan
pour one laddle of the mix in the pan and turn the pan to coer evenly the the pan
cook on high temperature for 2 min
turn the crepe
cook for another minutes

eat with sugar and butter or cheese and ham or any left over of your fridge
